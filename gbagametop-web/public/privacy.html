GBAGame Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

GBAGame is a GBA homebrew emulator app for discovering legal homebrew games and playing your own legally obtained game files.

Data We Store On Your Device

GBAGame stores app preferences, downloaded homebrew game records, imported game records, save files, and emulator state files locally on your device. These files are used for app functionality and quick replay.

Imported Files

When you import a .gba or .zip file, the file stays on your device. We do not upload your imported game files, save files, or emulator states to our servers.

Network Requests

The app connects to gbagametop.shop to load the legal homebrew game catalog, images, download links, and community link.

Advertising

GBAGame uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google and its partners may collect device identifiers such as the Android Advertising ID, ad interaction data, approximate location derived from IP address, and diagnostic data for advertising, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

Learn more about Google's advertising data practices: Google Ads Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by service providers such as Google AdMob as described above.

Data Retention And Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. You can remove imported games from inside the app where available.

Children

GBAGame is not designed for children under 13. If you are under the age required in your country, use the app only with permission from a parent or guardian.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer listed on the GBAGame Google Play store listing.
